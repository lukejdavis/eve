Glossary

Term Description
Alloy Resources used in research, important in early and mid game, less important once most research has been completed
Gas Critical for building components which are used to repair fleets. There is never a point in the game where this resource isn't important
Ice/Ore Resources which are used in both research and ship components
ISK In-game money. Used to upgrade advanced structures, ship traits and in some research structures to upgrade commanders
Navigation Bots Can be initiated by Military Commanders, Directors and CEO's of corporations. They travel from a starting point (grid must be owned by corp) to a final destination along a track between gates and citadels. Captures all grids along the way to the destination or when defeated by an enemy
Nova Credits In-game money. Used to purchase commander boxes, items from the interbus and research market and Navigation Bots. Can be purchased with quantum credits or won as event rewards and capture rewards
Quantum Credits In-game money. Used to purchase Nova credits, can only be purchased with money.
RSS Resources. Applies generally to Ice, Ore, Alloy and Gas but may include ISK
