Basics

1. The top left section details basic summary information on the commander. The top row is the name of the faction and name of the commander. Faction is important when constructing a Fleet. The second row shows the formations which this commander will give bonus's too. Lastly the icons here can be clicked and indicate what use the commander can be used for. Options include uses such as Offence, Support, Drones and repair.

2. This shows the current rank of the commander. This can be increased using the Promote button.

It also shows the Command Matrix active icon which indicates the Command Matrix is unlocked (3 Star rank). This gives an extra 300 squads to the fleet and +20 stamina.

3. Here you can find the basic attributes of the commanders.

Attribute Description
Squad Capacity In a fleet the number of squads is determined by the ship depending on research (Control Tower) and the commanders. If your reseach on control tower is 10/10 (10,000 squads) Then adding a level 1 commander will give 10,100 squads. Two level 1 commanders will give a squad capacity of 20,200 squads. Each commander level gives an extra 100 squads for a total of 50 levels and 5000 squads. Promotion level will also increase squad capacity. With unpromoted commanders, two max level commanders will give a total squad capacity of 30,000
Data Rate Determines the order a commander will fire, the higher the better. See Fleets

Note: At present Data Rate is supreme. If you fire first you kill first which means the enemy has less ships to fire back at you. This also includes getting off effects such as ECM Jam (Prevents other player from firing) or Force Shield (reduces incomming damage). The author highly recomends keeping this in mind when choosing commanders, especially those in a support role.
Offense This gives a 5% bonus to damage for every 100 offence. In the above picture Cerb has an offence of 586 which is roughly equivilent to 29.3% damage bonus. It also applies to some talents as well which is usually in the talent comments.

Note: This value ONLY applies from the primary commander. Secondary commander Offence and Tactics are ignored. Generally choose the commander with the best Offence and Tactics for the role the fleet requires.
Tactics This gives a 5% bonus to resistance for every 100 tactics. In the above picture Cerb has tactics of 327 which is roughly equivilent to 16.3% resistance. It also applies to some talents as well which is usually in the talent comments.

Note: Resistance is a damage reduction percentage. If you get hit by 100 damage, 20% resistance means only 80 damage gets through. HP on the ship is more like health. If you get hit by 80 damage after resistance then your HP drops by 80.

Note: This value ONLY applies from the primary commander. Secondary commander Offence and Tactics are ignored. Generally choose the commander with the best Offence and Tactics for the role the fleet requires.

4. Commander Talents. These determine what type of ship module will be used. They roughly fall into four categories. Weapon, Repair, Distruptor or Support. Weapons generally go boom boom but may include things like drones which act a little like a 2nd ship. Repair will repair damaged, but not destroyed, ships during battle. Disrupters are things like ECM jammers or neural shutdowns, these will cause a ship or commander to fail to use their traits and abilities. Support includes things like increased firepower, armor or quick response.

Commander talents cost cards to enable. To raise their levels simply level the commander. You will get 1 talent point for each level gained. Promotion will also give extra talent points. For the card requirements to enable:

Tier 1st Talent 2nd Talent 3rd Talent
Gold Free 40 Purple 40 Gold
Purple Free 40 Purple 40 Purple

Promote

The Promote screen is fairly self explanitory. There are 5 stars with 5 arms each. Each promotion will cost you a certain amount of BP's per arm. Initially the cost is 2 bp per arm, increasing to 4bp on the 5th arm. For each star it requires these number of BP's

  • 1 Star - 12bp's
  • 2 Star - 33bp's
  • 3 Star - 68bp's
  • 4 Star - 153bp's
  • 5 Star - 300bp's
The line driectly underneith shows what attribute will be increased with the next arm. When getting 5 arms (a star) the commander will unlock an extra talent or gain more talent points.

The Legendary Data Chip can also be swapped for a commander blueprints by hitting the plus button. These chips come from events, corp rewards or pirates.
